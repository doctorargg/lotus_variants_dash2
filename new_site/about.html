About Lotus Direct Care

Our mission is to restore the doctor-patient relationship through personalized, accessible healthcare

Our Practice

A new approach to primary care that puts patients first

Lotus Direct Care was founded on the belief that healthcare should be personal, accessible, and affordable. As a Direct Primary Care practice, we've removed the barriers that often stand between patients and their doctor, creating a healthcare experience that truly serves your needs.

Our practice is built on the principle that the doctor-patient relationship is sacred and should be the foundation of good healthcare. By limiting our patient panel size and eliminating insurance bureaucracy, we're able to provide the kind of personalized care that everyone deserves.

At Lotus Direct Care, you'll never feel rushed during appointments, struggle to reach your doctor, or be surprised by unexpected bills. Instead, you'll experience healthcare the way it should be—focused on you.

Meet Dr. Aaron Rosenberg

Physician Dedicated to Personalized Care

Dr. Rosenberg founded Lotus Direct Care with a vision to restore the doctor-patient relationship and provide truly personalized healthcare. With over 15 years of experience in primary care and functional medicine, he takes the time to listen, understand your concerns, and develop comprehensive treatment plans tailored to your unique needs.

Dr. Rosenberg's approach combines the best of conventional medicine with evidence-based functional medicine practices, addressing not just symptoms but the root causes of health issues. He believes in empowering patients through education and partnership, working together to achieve optimal health outcomes.

When not caring for patients, Dr. Rosenberg enjoys hiking, reading, and spending time with his family. He is actively involved in the community and regularly participates in local health education initiatives.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our practice

Relationship-Centered Care

We believe that the doctor-patient relationship is the foundation of good healthcare. By taking the time to truly know our patients, we can provide more personalized and effective care.

Our limited patient panel ensures that Dr. Rosenberg can develop meaningful relationships with each person under his care.

Compassionate Service

Healthcare should be delivered with empathy and understanding. We treat each patient with the compassion and respect they deserve, recognizing that illness can be both physically and emotionally challenging.

Our team approaches every interaction with kindness and a genuine desire to help.

Root Cause Focus

Rather than simply treating symptoms, we strive to identify and address the underlying causes of health issues. This approach leads to more effective, long-lasting solutions and better overall health.

We combine conventional medicine with functional medicine principles to provide comprehensive care.

Patient Education

We believe in empowering patients through education. When you understand your health condition and treatment options, you can make informed decisions and take an active role in your healthcare.

Dr. Rosenberg takes the time to explain complex medical concepts in accessible terms.

Preventive Approach

Prevention is always better than cure. We emphasize proactive health measures, regular screenings, and lifestyle modifications that can prevent disease before it starts.

Our comprehensive annual physicals and ongoing health monitoring help catch potential issues early.

Transparency

We believe in complete transparency in all aspects of our practice, from pricing to medical decision-making. You'll never encounter surprise bills or hidden fees at Lotus Direct Care.

Our membership model provides predictable costs and clear expectations.
