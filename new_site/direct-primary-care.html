What is Direct Primary Care?

A membership-based healthcare model that eliminates the middleman

Direct Primary Care (DPC) is an innovative healthcare model that removes insurance companies from the doctor-patient relationship. Instead of billing insurance, DPC practices charge a transparent monthly membership fee that covers all or most primary care services.

This approach allows us to spend more time with each patient, offer same-day or next-day appointments, and provide care at a fraction of the cost of traditional practices. It's healthcare the way it should be—focused on you, not paperwork.

Benefits of Direct Primary Care

Why our members love the DPC model

Extended Visits

Enjoy unhurried appointments that typically last 30-60 minutes, giving you ample time to discuss all your health concerns. No more feeling rushed through a 7-minute visit.

Same/Next-Day Appointments

Get care when you need it with same-day or next-day appointments for acute issues. No more waiting weeks to see your doctor when you're sick.

Direct Access

Communicate directly with your doctor via phone, text, email, or video chat. No more navigating phone trees or waiting for callbacks from nurses.

Transparent Pricing

Enjoy predictable monthly membership fees with no copays, deductibles, or surprise bills. Know exactly what you're paying for healthcare.

Wholesale Labs & Medications

Access lab tests and medications at or near wholesale prices, often saving 70-90% compared to traditional healthcare settings.

Personalized Care

Receive care from a doctor who knows you personally and has time to address your unique health needs and goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Direct Primary Care

While DPC covers your primary care needs, we recommend maintaining some form of coverage for catastrophic events, specialist care, and hospitalization. Many of our patients pair their DPC membership with:

  • A high-deductible health plan (HDHP)
  • A health sharing ministry
  • A health savings account (HSA)

We're happy to discuss options that might work best for your situation during your meet and greet.

Your membership includes:

  • Unlimited office visits with no copays
  • Annual comprehensive physical
  • Same-day or next-day appointments for acute issues
  • Telemedicine visits (phone, video, text, email)
  • Basic in-office procedures
  • Chronic disease management
  • Preventive care and wellness planning
  • Coordination with specialists when needed
  • Access to wholesale pricing on labs and medications

Some services may incur additional costs, such as certain procedures, labs, and medications, but these are always provided at or near wholesale prices with complete transparency.

Currently, IRS regulations are unclear regarding the use of HSA funds for DPC membership fees. Some patients choose to use their HSA, while others pay directly. We recommend consulting with your tax advisor for guidance specific to your situation.

There is legislation pending that would explicitly allow HSA funds to be used for DPC memberships, and we're hopeful this will pass in the near future.

While we can handle approximately 80-90% of your healthcare needs, there are times when specialist care is necessary. In these cases:

  • We'll refer you to trusted specialists in our network
  • We'll help coordinate your care and communicate with specialists
  • Your insurance would typically cover specialist visits according to your plan's terms
  • We'll help you interpret specialist findings and incorporate them into your overall care plan

Our goal is to serve as your healthcare advocate and coordinator, ensuring you receive the best possible care even when it extends beyond our practice.

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time with 30 days' notice. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. We believe in earning your trust and business each month.

If you need to cancel due to financial hardship or other circumstances, please talk to us. We're committed to working with our patients to ensure they receive the care they need.
