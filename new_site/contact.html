Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I schedule a Meet & Greet?

You can schedule a free Meet & Greet by clicking the "Schedule a Meet & Greet" button on our website, or by calling our office directly at (555) 123-4567.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID, a list of current medications, and any recent lab results or medical records you'd like to discuss. If you have insurance for specialist referrals, bring your insurance card as well.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

As a member, you can typically get same-day or next-day appointments for urgent needs, and appointments within a week for non-urgent matters.

Do you offer virtual visits?

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for appropriate situations. These are included in your membership at no additional cost.
