Primary Care

Included in Membership

Comprehensive primary care services for patients of all ages. We take the time to listen to your concerns and develop personalized treatment plans that address your unique health needs.

  • Annual Physical Exams: Comprehensive yearly check-ups with thorough health assessments
  • Acute Care Visits: Same or next-day appointments for illnesses and injuries
  • Chronic Disease Management: Ongoing care for conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and asthma
  • Preventive Screenings: Age and risk-appropriate health screenings
  • Basic Office Procedures: Including skin biopsies, joint injections, and minor wound care
  • Health Coaching: Guidance on lifestyle modifications to improve health outcomes
  • Medication Management: Regular review and optimization of medications

Unlike traditional primary care practices where appointments are rushed and often delayed, at Lotus Direct Care:

  • Your appointments are 30-60 minutes long, not the typical 7-15 minutes
  • You'll see your doctor, not a rotating cast of providers
  • Same or next-day appointments when you're sick
  • Direct communication with your doctor via phone, text, or email
  • No rushing through your concerns or feeling like "just another patient"
  • Comprehensive care that addresses root causes, not just symptoms

Preventive Care

Included in Membership

Proactive healthcare focused on preventing illness and maintaining optimal health through regular screenings, assessments, and personalized wellness strategies.

  • Comprehensive Health Risk Assessments: Detailed evaluation of your health risks based on personal and family history
  • Age-Appropriate Screenings: Cancer screenings, cardiovascular assessments, and other preventive tests
  • Immunizations: Up-to-date vaccinations based on age and risk factors
  • Personalized Wellness Plans: Customized strategies to optimize your health
  • Nutrition Counseling: Evidence-based dietary guidance for your specific needs
  • Stress Management: Techniques and resources to reduce stress and improve mental wellbeing
  • Sleep Optimization: Assessment and strategies for better sleep quality

At Lotus Direct Care, we believe that preventing disease is always better than treating it. Our preventive approach includes:

  • Taking time to understand your unique health risks
  • Creating personalized prevention strategies
  • Regular monitoring of key health indicators
  • Early intervention when warning signs appear
  • Empowering you with knowledge and tools for optimal health
  • Addressing lifestyle factors that impact long-term health

Chronic Disease Management

Included in Membership

Comprehensive care for ongoing health conditions with a focus on improving quality of life, preventing complications, and addressing root causes rather than just managing symptoms.

  • Diabetes: Type 1, Type 2, and prediabetes
  • Cardiovascular Conditions: Hypertension, high cholesterol, heart disease
  • Respiratory Disorders: Asthma, COPD
  • Thyroid Disorders: Hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism
  • Autoimmune Conditions: Rheumatoid arthritis, lupus, Hashimoto's
  • Digestive Issues: IBS, GERD, Crohn's disease
  • Mental Health: Depression, anxiety, ADHD
  • Metabolic Disorders: Metabolic syndrome, PCOS

Our approach to chronic disease management goes beyond prescription medications to address underlying causes:

  • Comprehensive initial assessment and regular monitoring
  • Personalized treatment plans that evolve with your condition
  • Integration of conventional and functional medicine approaches
  • Focus on lifestyle modifications that improve outcomes
  • Regular follow-up to adjust treatments as needed
  • Coordination with specialists when necessary
  • Patient education and self-management support
